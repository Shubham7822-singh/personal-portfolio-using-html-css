Quiz Game Project:

The Quiz Game is a web-based interactive application designed to engage users by testing their knowledge across various topics. Built using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, the game offers a dynamic and user-friendly interface, allowing players to select categories and difficulty levels. The game displays multiple-choice questions and provides immediate feedback on the correctness of answers. Scores are tracked throughout the session, motivating users to improve with each attempt. This project showcases proficiency in front-end development, emphasizing responsive design, interactive elements, and seamless user experience.

  • Multiple categories and difficulty levels.
  • Instant feedback on answers.
  • Score tracking and progress display.


E-commerce website:

Designed and developed a fully functional e-commerce website aimed at enhancing the online shopping experience. The platform allows users to browse, search, and purchase products seamlessly. It features a user-friendly interface, secure payment processing, and efficient order management, making it a reliable choice for both consumers and merchants.

  • User-Friendly Interface: Intuitive navigation and responsive design ensure a smooth shopping experience on any device.
  • Product Catalog: Allows users to explore a wide range of products with detailed descriptions, images, and reviews.
  • Shopping Cart and Checkout: Easy-to-use shopping cart functionality, coupled with secure payment options, including credit/debit cards and digital wallets.
